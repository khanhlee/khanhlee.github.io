How to pursue a Ph.D. degree? Is Ph.D. a long and difficult process? They are the common questions of almost students who are considering whether to pursue a Ph.D. degree or not. I am currently a graduate student in Department of Computer Science and Engineering at YZU who conducted the research on bioinformatics, big data and data analyst. I will graduate this year and I will share information about my experience in Ph.D. studies. Do you feel prepared for Ph.D. life, ready for scientific research and step into the scientific world? Here I will focus on sharing my knowledge and experiences from one who had no knowledge in Ph.D. and scientific research. Second, I would like to share the detail about scientific research and applications in computer science, bioinformatics, big data, … You will understand the steps to conduct a research as well as dissertation thesis. A vital part worth talking is about conferences and journals, such as identification, writing, submitting, and publishing. In the final section, I would like to introduce our lab research fields and future research orientations.
